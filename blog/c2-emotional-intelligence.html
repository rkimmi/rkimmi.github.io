Emotional Intelligence

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

Connections between self-motivation, self-regulation and emotional intelligence seem obvious now but were not too obvious to me beforehand. I had mostly understood emotional intelligence to be more of an interpersonal need than an intrapersonal one, although that does seem to not make much sense when thinking about it now.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

Pretty helpful, although I spent about five minutes trying to 'clear my mind' and that didn't work. This did bring my attention to realising the kind of thoughts that often distract me, so that may be a good first step.
