Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I have tried the pomodoro technique which felt a bit strange initially, but I understand how and why it's useful; I think I'll practice this more. I've also become enthusiastic about toggle and have been using it to timebox all of my projects including those outside of Dev.

What did you find interesting?

I found the thinking around building good daily habits pretty interesting.

What worked for you?

Working on dev work a little bit everyday — previously I have been scheduling in Dev work for a couple of days a week when I was able to commit some hours at a time. I found through doing this that I had to revise a lot of the concepts I had learnt previously when revisiting material and was unorganised. This made a lot of my committed time to dev somewhat unproductive. I've found that even just reading over the material if I'm too tired at the end of the day has proven useful.

Which will you experiment further with?

I will continue experimenting with the pomodoro technique as I feel it probably will improve my concentration over time.
