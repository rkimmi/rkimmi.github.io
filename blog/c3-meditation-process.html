Annoying at first, as maybe I didn't understand the point of the excercise, was focussed on the product and rejected simply following the guidlines. What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I had some more basic understanding about how and why meditation works — but have thought that I was/am just not easily able to do it. Previous recent attempts at meditating had me either fall asleep, or develop a headache.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

The incredible positive influence of meditation on memory and other brain functions wasn't completely new information, but Chapter 2 of 'Search Inside Yourself' explained the studies and findings of this in somewhat simple terms, I guess I have a more clear understanding of this now.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

Meditating with my eyes open was super helpful. I didn't fall asleep (good!), and managed to notice when my thoughts drifted more readily.
